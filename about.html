Our Vision: To make design easier and more accessible to everyone

Rendyr started with the vision of Kaelum Hasler, a long time tinkerer and a student of industrial design at Virginia Tech. While building his design portfolio, Kaelum discovered that rapid prototyping technologies like 3D printers, laser cutters, and CNC machines were much less accessible to him than he would have liked. Inspired, he began his own research and started to put together a team that could solve the problems of affordability, portability, versatility, and sustainability of these machines. From these principles Rendyr was formed as a company that develops creative solutions to make design easier and more accessible to everyone.

Our first project, Optic, is a laptop version of an industrial laser cutter.
