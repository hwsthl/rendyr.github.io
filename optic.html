Optic is the portable laser cutter that will revolutionize how we think about rapid prototyping.

Optic is our very own portable laser cutter capable of cutting and engraving wood, paper, cardboard, acrylic, fabric, and more. It is equipped with a 40 Watt laser, giving it both the speed and precision of an industrial grade machine. Our revolutionary folding technology allows it to have a profile 10% of the size of traditional machines while maintaining an 18”x24” cut volume. Because the Optic has a smaller form factor than our leading competitors while still delivering a larger cut area it is the perfect choice for your day to day laser needs.
